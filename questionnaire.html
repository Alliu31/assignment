QUESTIONNAIRE FOR PROSTATE DIAGNOSING

Do you have difficulty starting to urinate?

Do you experience a weak urine stream?

Do you feel like your bladder isn't empty after urinating?

Do you have to urinate frequently, especially at night (nocturia)?

Do you experience urinary urgency or a sudden urge to urinate?

Do you have pain or discomfort during urination?

Have you noticed blood in your urine?

Do you have a history of prostate problems or family history of prostate issues?

Have you experienced any sexual dysfunction, such as erectile dysfunction or decreased libido?

Have you had any recent urinary tract infections or kidney problems?

Have you noticed that your urine stream stops and starts several times during urination?

Do you feel an urgent need to urinate immediately after finishing?

Have you had any issues with urinary incontinence (unintentional loss of urine)?

Do you feel a burning sensation when you urinate?

Have you experienced any changes in the color or smell of your urine?
